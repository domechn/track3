✨ Features

Everything you need to track your portfolio

From exchange balances to broker positions and on-chain wallets, Track3 brings your portfolio into one private desktop workspace.

🔒

100% Local & Encrypted

All data is stored locally in an encrypted SQLite database. No servers, no cloud. Your API keys and wallet addresses never leave your device.

📈

Rich Analytics & Charts

Dozens of charts for allocation, PnL, top movers, wallet breakdowns, historical performance, and more.

🔗

Exchanges, Brokers & Wallets

Connect centralized exchanges with read-only API keys, import Interactive Brokers positions, and track on-chain wallets in one place.

💰

Profit & Loss Tracking

Track your realized and unrealized PnL with cost-basis calculations, asset change history, and ATH (all-time-high) monitoring.

📦

Backup & Restore

Export your data to local files and import it on another device. Keep machines in sync with iCloud, Dropbox, or any cloud drive.

⛓

No Wallet Signature Required

Track3 only reads public on-chain data and exchange balances. It never asks you to sign a transaction or grant fund access.
